MCPA STL CTF 2015

Register Now!

Register for the 2015 MCPA Capture the Flag! Compete against other teams to solve cyber security challenges to find hidden flags. The team with the most flags at the end will be declared the winner! No cyber security skills? No problem, hints and mentors will be available throughout the competition. While mentors are free, the hints will cost you points.

Please note: if you will not be at our on-site location for the competition please select the "Remote" bracket when registering. Remote competition will be free of charge but please be advised you will not be eligible to receive the local cash prizes. There will be a special prize for the winner of the remote bracket. See our About page for more details.

*While your password should be kept a secret and only used by you, your Team Passphrase is shared with the other members of your team. If you forget your Team Passphrase, it will be displayed on the "Team" page after you log in.

Note that while Teacher Accounts can solve challenges during the competition, they do not appear on the public scoreboard.

* This event is free for MCPA Members. Interested in membership? US Government Employees and Veterans may qualify for a free lifetime membership! See public.milcyber.org/join. Contact us for coupon code.
